Beef Pie

An Aussie icon, this classic pie is made with a shortcrust base and a rich beef mince gravy.

Ingredients

  • 1 x Four'n Twenty Pies Meat 4 Pack
  • 1 x oven
  • 1 x baking tray
  • 1 x sheet of baking paper

Method

  1. Place baking paper on tray and remove pies from packet
  2. Place pie on top of baking sheet and place in the oven at 180C for 20 minutes
  3. Once cooked remove pie from oven and eat with some dead horse
