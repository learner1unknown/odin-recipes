Fairy Bread

An Aussie kids dinner.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Butter
  • Multicoloured Sprinkles

Method

  1. Spread Butter on a slice of white bread
  2. Sprinkle the buttered slice of white bread with more sprinkles than is possible
  3. Shake off excess sprinkles

This all should cost $2.00 AUD, bloody amazing!
