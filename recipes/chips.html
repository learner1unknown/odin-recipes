Chips

The perfect addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 x Pack of frozen chips
  • 1 x oven
  • 1 x baking tray
  • 1 x sheet of baking paper

Method

  1. Place baking paper on tray and remove chips from packet
  2. Place chips on top of baking sheet and place in the oven at 200C for 15 minutes
  3. Once cooked remove chips from oven and eat with some dead horse and anything else of your choosing
